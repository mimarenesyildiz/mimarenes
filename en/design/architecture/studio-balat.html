Interior Design 2024

Industrial Conversion

Studio Balat

Balat, Istanbul — 1,200 m²

An industrial hangar of roughly 1,200 m² in Balat was converted into the head office of a production company. The core idea of the design is to preserve the impressive volume and daylight potential the building offers, while integrating the flexible and hierarchical spaces demanded by modern working culture within this shell.

Structural Strategy

Preserving the rhythm of the existing steel trusses and the industrial character, a steel mezzanine was added to the space. This intervention nearly doubled the usable area while maintaining the vertical continuity and visual depth of the space.

Corridor Gallery

By tucking ventilation ducts and services into the adjacent volumes, the main ground-floor corridor was kept at maximum height — no longer a mere passage, it became a gallery displaying production posters.

A strip of timber flooring running the length of the corridor traces a warm line of direction within the dark epoxy floor.

Material Palette

The brutalist presence of black steel is balanced by natural timber stair details and warm floor finishes. The contrast of raw and refined textures creates a dynamic working atmosphere, while three core materials—black-painted steel, natural oak and glass—repeat at different scales to give the space its coherence.

Steel shelving takes on different characters throughout the project according to storage and display needs: a space divider in the open office, bookshelves in the directors' rooms, display units in the social areas.

Natural Light

Gallery voids and skylights allow daylight to reach the very center of the interior despite the depth of the building. Light moves through the building over the course of the day; one side of the corridor brightens in the morning, the other in the afternoon. This shifting atmosphere creates a living space rather than a static office.

Outcome

Studio Balat proposes reinterpretation over preservation: industrial heritage meets a new function. The result is a flexible, inspiring working environment that supports the creative energy of a production company.
