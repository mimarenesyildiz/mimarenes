Stone Façade Fabrication Documentation

Victory Park Housing Complex

Moscow, Russia — Ul. Bratiev Fonchenko

For the housing complex designed by Sergey Skuratov Architects facing Moscow's Victory Park (Park Pobedy), I prepared the stone fabrication and assembly documentation for the façade works. A total of 7,436 technical drawings — covering cutting dimensions, profile details and assembly diagrams for the horizontal and vertical stone panels of all five residential towers — were generated with parametric algorithms I developed in Rhino Grasshopper.

The Parametric Approach

The parametric system automatically generated the unique dimensions, edge profiles and position codes of every stone panel, minimizing production errors. Façade rhythms and window layouts that vary from block to block were managed through a single algorithmic infrastructure.

This approach dramatically shortened a process that would have taken months with conventional drafting, and delivered high precision in both production and assembly.
